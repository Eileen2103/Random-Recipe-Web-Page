Rainbow Cheesecake

Ingredients
Rainbow sprinkles from the end of the rainbow.
Pink Lily Extract
Cloud Powders
Easter Eggs(3)

Instructions
1- Crack the eggs and mix well.
2- Add cloud powder while mixing.
3-Add 2-3 drops of pink lily extract.
4-Bake the cake mixture in the oven for 30-45 minutes at maximum heat.
5-Finally let the cake cool for an hour and add rainbow sprinkles on top.

Unicorn Tear Drop Cake

Ingredients
Unicorn tears (very very very rare).
Vanilla extract from the Smurfs Market
3/4 cup of water from Paradise Falls

Instructions
1- Add 4-5 drops of Unicorn tears to your mixing bowl.
2- Add vanilla extract and water then mix well.
3-Pour mixture into molds.
4-Place molds into the fridge to set for 10 hours.

Gold Honey Popsicle

Ingredients
300 g of gold honey from the Yellow Forest(Be aware of the bee colonies).
100 g of Gelatin from the lotuses of the Crystalstar Lake
3/4 cup of water from Paradise Falls

Instructions
1- Add the gelatin and water into a pan and cook for about 10 minutes until it gets creamy.
2- Let the gelatin mix cool while you put honey into a golden bowl for the honey to release it's golden color.
3-Add the honey to gelatin mix and pour mixture into molds.
4-Place molds into the fridge to set for 10 hours.
